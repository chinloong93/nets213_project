How To Contribute To The Txt Help Hotline

If you would like us to craft a response to your text message:
1. Simply text 'Help' to 1-267-460-0904
2. Follow our systems steps and wait for us to text you back the message

If you would like to help contribute responses to other people's text messages:
1. Go to https://www.reddit.com/r/txthotline/
2. Sign in or sign up for reddit
3. Navigate to a text request post and look at the message they need a response to
4. You may upvote existing comments if you think their suggested responses are good or comment your own response
